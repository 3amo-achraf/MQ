Heritage of Craft

MQ is built on a foundation of precision manufacturing, artisanal finishing, and timeless design. Every component is selected and applied to ensure a premium tactile and visual experience.

The brand honors the discipline of traditional watchmakers while pushing the boundaries of contemporary luxury. This results in watches that are technically superior and unmistakably elegant.

Signature Processes

Movement assembly

Precision engine parts are assembled by hand and tested for stability, performance, and exceptional accuracy.

Dial creation

Each dial is created with layered finishes, applied indexes, and meticulous color depth to ensure a luxurious presence.

Final refinement

Final polishing, assembly, and quality inspection are carried out by experienced watchmakers to deliver a flawless finished product.

Materials & Finish

Gold & Titanium

Premium metals combine strength and luster to deliver striking elegance with enduring wear.

Sapphire Crystal

Scratch-resistant and crystal-clear, the sapphire glass protects your watch with durable luxury.

Leather & Ceramic

Refined leather straps and precision ceramic bracelets elevate comfort and visual contrast.

Why MQ

Purposeful luxury

Every MQ watch is created with an emphasis on utility, clarity, and enduring style.

Exclusive experience

MQ customers enjoy premium service, direct access to limited releases, and guided purchase support.
